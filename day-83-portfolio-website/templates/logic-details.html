Full System Logic & Simulation

The filtration system operates in two primary modes: Normal Filtration and Backwash. The logic is implemented in RSLogix 500 with emulation.

P&ID Breakdown

Instruments: Flow switch (FS1), pressure transmitters (PT1, PT2), level transmitter (LT1).
Valves: Solenoid valves (SV1–SV6), manual ball valve (BV1).
Pump (P1): Circulates water, managed by PLC.

Normal Filtration Mode

SV1 opens, Pump 1 runs, water filtered → stored in Clean Water Tank via SV5.

Backwash Mode

On high ΔP: SV1 closes, SV2 opens, Pump 1 reverses flow to clean filter → contaminants drained.
